Lee Electronics Policies:

1. Warranty Policy:
   - All products come with a 1-year limited warranty.
   - The warranty covers manufacturing defects only.
   - Physical damage or misuse voids the warranty.

2. Return Policy:
   - Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
   - Products must be in original packaging with all accessories.
   - Proof of purchase is required.

3. Privacy Policy:
   - We do not share customer information with third parties.
   - Customer data is encrypted and stored securely.
   - You may request deletion of your data by contacting support.

4. Delivery Policy:
   - Standard delivery: 3-5 business days.
   - Express delivery: 1-2 business days (extra fee applies).
   - Delivery delays due to weather or logistics will be communicated proactively.

5. Payment Policy:
   - Accepted methods: Visa, MasterCard, EcoCash, ZIPIT, and bank transfers.
   - Orders must be paid in full before dispatch.
   - Refunds are processed within 7 working days after approval.

6. Exchange Policy:
   - Exchanges are allowed within 7 days of purchase.
   - Only defective or unopened items are eligible.
